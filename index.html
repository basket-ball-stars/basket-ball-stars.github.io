BASKETBALL STARS: PLAY UNBLOCKED FREE UNLIMITED

Basketball Stars is a basketball game that has always maintained its popularity. Featuring a real-time multiplayer mode, this game allows you to enjoy fast-paced and fun basketball matches. You can choose different characters and test your skills in one-on-one matches. The games are usually short, which ensures high-paced action at every moment of the game.

The main objective of Basketball Stars is to outscore your opponent by making more baskets and winning the game in the end. Players can move freely on the court, steal the ball, block, and shoot on offense. Timing is crucial when shooting; you can make more accurate shots by releasing the shot meter at the right point. Additionally, while defending, you can watch your opponent's movements and gain an advantage with blocks or steal attempts.

Basketball Stars Controls: How to Play?

First Player Keys:

Movement: W: Up - A: Left - S: Down - D: Right

Shooting / Blocking: B Key

Stealing / Speed Boost: V Key

Second Player Keys:

Movement: Arrow Keys: Up, Down, Left, Right

Shooting / Blocking: L Key

Stealing / Speed Boost: K Key
